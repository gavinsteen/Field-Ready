Coaching Excellence for Technical People

Field Readys Coaching Excellence training programme aims to help technical managers who are in supervisory positions gain the skills needed to coach, manage, and develop junior staff.
The course is instructor-led, with live, online learning sessions once a week for 9 weeks and is flexible to fit into the shift patterns and busy work schedules of participants. It follows a unique learn/experience/reflect model that creates interactive learning opportunities for trainees and builds feedback and accountability into the learning journey. Alongside weekly assignments, trainees are required to complete two Coaching Experiences, giving them the chance to apply what they have learned. At the end of the course, every trainee develops a Coaching Plan setting out how they intend to implement what was learned going forward. We have worked with more than 30 companies to train and empower their technical people to become brilliant managers, building better businesses in the process.
